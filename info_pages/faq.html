General FAQs

  1. What is this? How does this work?

    SpaceLearn is an AI-powered learning platform that personalizes your study experience.
    It creates custom learning paths based on your strengths, weaknesses, and goals.
    You can track progress, take quizzes, and explore curated resources to master any topic.

  2. Is SpaceLearn free to use?

    Yes! SpaceLearn is completely free to use at the moment.

  3. What topics can I learn on SpaceLearn?

    Anything you want! We support all kinds of topics, ranging from science, literature, languages, history, and so on.
    The website curates its content from various trusted sources to enhance your learning journey.

Course Creation FAQs

  1. How do I create a course?

    Simply go to your Dashboard, click on "Create Course", enter the subject details, and start adding learning materials.
    The AI will suggest structured lessons based on your inputs.

  2. Can I edit / delete a course after it is created?

    Of course! You can update your course at any time from the Course Management section. If you wish to delete a course,
    just select the course and click the "Delete" button.

  3. How does course progression work?

    As you complete lessons and quizzes, your course "planet" evolves, symbolizing your mastery of the topic.
    Based on your engagement with the material, you will see your planet evolve into further stages.

AI-Related FAQs

  1. How does the AI determine my learning path?

    The AI uses a placement test to assess your current knowledge level. Based on your answers, it designs a personalized
    course that focuses on strengthening weak areas while reinforcing what you already know.

  2. Can the AI adapt my learning path over time?

    Yes! The AI continuously analyzes your progress and adjusts the difficulty of lessons and quizzes. If you excel, it will
    introduce advanced concepts. If you struggle, it will reinforce key topics before moving forward.

  3. What sources does the AI use to generate learning material?

    SpaceLearn curates content from Khan Academy, Coursera, Udemy, YouTube, research papers, and more. The AI filters the best
    resources based on your learning preferences and study goals.

Technical FAQs

  1. What are the system requirements to use SpaceLearn?

    SpaceLearn runs directly in your browser, so you don't have to worry about any downloads. All you need is a stable internet connection.

  2. Is my data secure on SpaceLearn?

    We take the security of your data very seriously. Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties.

  3. Will SpaceLearn have a mobile app in the future?

    Very possible... Be on the lookout for that.
