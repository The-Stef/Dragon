About The Website

SpaceLearn's objective is to foster a new method of learning information, leveraging new technological advancements in the domain of Artificial Intelligence.

The Problem

Traditional OLPs often provide one-size-fits-all content, which often falls short of the expectations that individual users have. Unmet expectations can result in a plethora of issues for the users, such as:

  • Frustration.
  • Disengagement with the content.
  • People might recommend against using your app to their mutuals.

The Solution

Our objective is to create a high quality website where users are free to learn any kind of subject they would wish to engage with. In order to provide our users with the best experience possible, we provide an ensemble of features, such as:

  • AI-Powered Knowledge Assistant
    • Taking a placement test to assess a user's current Knowledge level
    • Examination of user's strengths and weaknesses
  • Goal Oriented Learning
    • Users can set specific learning goals (i.e. “Reach B1 level German in 5 months”)
    • The system automatically structures a study plan to achieve the goals
  • AI-Generated Tests & Quizzes
    • Speed Repetition methods inspired by Duolingo's strategy
    • Flashcards
    • Quizzes
    • Leveraging AI "Deep Research" options
